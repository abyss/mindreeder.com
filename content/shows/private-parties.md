## The Private Experience

The intimacy of close-up work produces effects no stage can replicate.

Craig's private party work is rooted in this intimacy. Moving through your event as a guest rather than a spectacle, he engages individuals and small groups with effects that feel personal, tailored, and entirely inexplicable. A playing card selected in silence. A word thought and never spoken, read aloud. A guest's childhood memory surfaced with startling accuracy.

### Formats

**Strolling Close-Up** — Craig circulates through your reception or dinner, spending time with each group. Guests are unaware of what to expect until it begins, which creates a cascade of wonder as word spreads through the room.

**Parlour Show** — A seated performance for larger groups, ideal as an after-dinner entertainment. Flexible in length, from fifteen to forty-five minutes, to suit your event, this format brings the intimacy of close-up work to a larger audience without losing its personal quality.

**Combination** — For longer events, Craig recommends opening the evening with strolling work during cocktail hour, then closing with a brief parlour set after dinner.

### Occasions

Craig performs for birthday milestones, anniversaries, wedding receptions, engagement parties, retirement celebrations, and any gathering where the host wants to offer their guests something genuinely unexpected.

All private engagements are handled with complete discretion.
