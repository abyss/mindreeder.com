## The Corporate Experience

Craig's corporate work starts from a simple premise: people remember how they felt, not what they heard. That spark of wonder, shared collectively across a room of colleagues and clients, creates a bond that no keynote or team-building exercise can manufacture.

His corporate programme is modular and fully adaptable. Whether he joins your event as an after-dinner headliner, a conference centrepiece, or a strolling close-up performer during a reception, he works with you in advance to shape the performance around your themes, your audience, and your goals.

### Formats

**Strolling Close-Up** — Craig circulates through your reception, networking session, or trade show floor, engaging guests in small groups with effects that are personal, surprising, and impossible to explain. Ideal for cocktail hours, exhibit halls, and any environment where guests are moving.

**Parlour or Stage Show** — A formal performance for seated audiences, flexible in length from fifteen to forty-five minutes to suit your programme. Works well as an after-dinner closer, a conference centrepiece, or a general session opener.

**Combination** — For longer events and conventions, Craig recommends strolling work during receptions or exhibit hours, followed by a stage set at a natural gathering point in the agenda.

**Convention & Trade Show** — Booth-based performance designed to draw foot traffic, hold attention, and create a memorable touchpoint for your brand. Craig performs in short, repeating sets that work for an ever-changing audience, with messaging woven naturally into the material.

### What to Expect

**Reliability** — Craig coordinates directly with your event team, arrives prepared, and respects your schedule. You will not be chasing him down on the day.

**Professionalism** — Every performance is conducted with discretion and care, reflecting well on you and your organization.

**Integration** — Craig matches the tone of your event, from attire to formality to energy, so he fits seamlessly into your team rather than standing apart from it.

**Brand Alignment** — Material can incorporate your company name, product messaging, or event themes naturally and without feeling forced.

**Adaptability** — If the schedule shifts or the room changes, Craig adjusts. The performance fits your event, not the other way around.

### Client Sectors

Craig is available for corporate engagements across any industry, including technology, finance, luxury goods, legal services, media, and beyond. As a technology consultant for enterprise customers, Craig brings an insider's familiarity with corporate audiences and the events they attend. He also attends trade shows regularly as a participant, and knows firsthand what it takes to draw attention on a busy show floor. His material is always appropriate and designed to delight the broadest possible cross-section of a professional audience.
