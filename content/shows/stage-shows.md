## The Stage Show

Craig's stage show is a structured performance built around a single idea: that the most interesting thing in the room is the audience itself.

Each show follows a narrative arc. The effects build on each other, shifting the audience's understanding of what is happening and why. By the end, the earlier moments reframe themselves in ways that weren't visible at the time.

### Format

Stage performances are available in sets from fifteen to seventy-five minutes, scaled to your venue and programme. Shorter sets work as a centrepiece within a larger event; the full seventy-five minutes is a complete standalone experience.

### Venues

Craig performs on theatre stages, concert halls, corporate atriums, and event spaces with a clear sightline to a stage area. Audience sizes from fifty to several hundred.
